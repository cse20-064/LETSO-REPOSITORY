Ojectives of the club

FILA fc is a youth development football team founded in 2013 by Coach Fila Anderson with the intension of moulding future Botswana football athletes .The goal is to establish early football experience for these young boys to ensure they cope with the current and future enviroment of the modern footballer in order to compete at the highest level.
